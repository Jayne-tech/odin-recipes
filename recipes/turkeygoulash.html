Turkey Goulash

Description

Turkey Goulash is a hearty and flavorful meal that combines the tender goodness of turkey with a rich, aromatic stew. Typically, it begins by sauteing turkey pieces in a mix of aromatic spices and herbs like paprika, garlic, and onion, creating a tantalizing base of flavors. This dish often features a medley of vegetables like bell peppers, tomatoes, and potatoes, adding both texture and nutritional value to the stew. As the ingredients meld together during the slimmering process, the turkey becomes succulent and infused with the essence of the spices, resulting in a satisfying and comforting meal.

The magic of turkey goulash lies in its versatility and ability to adapt to various tastes and preferences. Some variations may incorporate additional ingredients like mushrooms, carrots, or even a touch of sour cream for creaminess. The stew's balance of flavors, combined with the tender turkey, offers a delightful experience for anyone seeking a wholesome and delicious dish that can be enjoyed in different settings, whether it's a cozy family dinner or a gathering of friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 carrots sliced
  • 3-4 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 cups beef or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saute onions and garlic.
  2. Add turkey pieces, browning them on all sides.
  3. Stir in spices like paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss in chopped vegetables like bell peppers and tomatoes.
  5. Pour in broth and let it simmer until turkey is tender.
  6. Adjust seasoning and thickness as desired.
  7. Serve hot, optionally garnished with fresh herbs.

    8. Enjoy your turkey goulash!
