Pizza Dough

Description

Pizza, a beloved Italian creation, starts with a thin, crispy dough base made of flour, water, yeast, salt, and olive oil. This versatile canvas is adorned with a classic combo: zesty tomato sauce and a generous blanket of gooey mozzarella cheese.

But, the magic truly happens with toppings, offering a symphony of flavors. From savory pepperoni to a colorful array of veggies or even a touch of aromatic basil, each bite is a burst of delight. Baked to perfection, pizza encapsulates a perfect balance of textures and tastes, making it a cherished favorite around the globe.

Ingredients

    For the dough:

  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 2 cups warm water
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

    • For the toppings:

  • 2 pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water and sugar. Sprinkle the yeast over the water and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the activated yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, or until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 475°F.
  5. Divide the dough in half for two pizzas. Roll out each portion on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  6. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  7. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozarella cheese over the sauce, and add your desired toppings.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  9. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
